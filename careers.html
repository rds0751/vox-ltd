Russian SpeakerBusiness Development Manager

Who we are:

We are an international Forex Broker with 15 years team experience, with rapidly expanding +100 employees, 5 offices around the globe and providing services in 8 Languages.

We are looking for Business Development Manager to develop the market in Russian speaking countries with the following duties and requirements

Job Description:

  • Conduct market research and create Business Plans to expand the business of the company in the target countries.
  • Getting on board new potential business partners and clients in the target countries.
  • Meet with customers/clients face to face or over the phone.
  • Understand the needs of customers and be able to respond effectively with a plan of how to meet those needs.
  • Establish strong partnerships to maximize sales in assigned regions.
  • help to grow the business with existing partners in assigned countries.
  • Account management of business partners including negotiating the terms of partnership.
  • Discuss marketing plans with Marketing Department for the assigned market.
  • Propose to line manager new potential products as per market trends and customer needs.
  • Attend seminars, conferences and events where needed and request by Company.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor degree (degrees in finance or business related will be considered an extra qualification point)
  • A Master degree is considered an extra qualification point.
  • Minimum 3 years working experience in a similar position.
  • Ability to travel extensively around the world to meet customers and participate in Exhibitions.
  • Fluent in Russian and English speaking, Other languages are considered as an advantage.
  • Microsoft office and computer skills.
  • Knowledge of CRM and other software is considered an advantage.
  • Knowledge and experience in financial markets and CFD trading.
  • Ability to relocate in other countries ( where company has office) based on company needs.

Remuneration Package Offered:

  • The position is full time and will be based in one of our offices in around the globe depending on the applicant's country of residence.
  • An attractive Remuneration Package including medical insurance and performance bonuses.
